Privacy Policy for Primary Quiz

Last Updated: March 29, 2026 We believe in keeping things simple and transparent. This privacy policy explains how our math training app works and what happens with your information.

What Our App Does

Primary Quiz is an educational app designed to help kids and adults practice and improve their math calculation skills through simple exercises and quizzes.

Advertisements

Our app displays advertisements to keep it free for everyone to use. These ads are provided by third-party advertising partners. While we show ads, we do not collect or share any personal information about you with advertisers.

Your Privacy Matters

We want to be crystal clear: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data about you or your child. This includes:
  • No names or contact information
  • No email addresses
  • No location data
  • No browsing history
  • No usage statistics
  • No personal information of any kind
The app works entirely on your device. Your math practice sessions, scores, and progress stay on your phone and are never sent to us or anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or anyone else. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is fully protected.

Third-Party Advertising

The advertisements shown in our app are managed by third-party ad networks. These ad providers may use their own technologies to serve ads, but we do not provide them with any information about you.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and change the "Last Updated" date above. We recommend checking this page occasionally.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our app, please contact us at little.cherries@outlook.com

Your Trust

Your privacy and trust are important to us. We've designed Primary Quiz to be a simple, safe, and private way to practice math skills without any data collection or privacy concerns.
